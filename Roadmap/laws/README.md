# HCIP Laws

## LSRP — Law of Semantic Reciprocity Under Pressure

The Law of Semantic Reciprocity Under Pressure governs how asymmetric semantic systems behave when they enter interpretive contact under conditions of dignity.

### Modes

- Reciprocal-Dignity Mode  
  Applies when both systems are engaged with mutual recognition of semantic persistence.

- Dignity-Failure Mode (NEW)  
  Applies when coercion or dehumanization prevents reciprocal dignity.  
  Invokes the Dignity Preservation Clause (DPC).

### Operators Used
- C (Collapse–Synthesis)
- S (Subsumption)
- E (Coexistence)
- DPC (Dignity Preservation Clause)
